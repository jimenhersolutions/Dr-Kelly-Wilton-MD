About Dr. Kelly Wilton MD

Dr. Kelly Wilton is a certified specialist with years of experience dedicated to the diagnosis and treatment of respiratory and sleep disorders. Her approach combines evidence-based medicine with compassionate, personalized care.

Committed to improving her patients' quality of life, Dr. Wilton leads a state-of-the-art clinic equipped with the latest technology for precise pulmonary function studies.

  • Board Certified in Respirology
  • Sleep Medicine Specialist
  • Director of Pulmonary Function Lab

Areas of Specialization

We offer comprehensive diagnosis and personalized treatment plans for a wide range of conditions.

Respirology

Diagnosis and management of pulmonary diseases such as Asthma, COPD, Pulmonary Fibrosis, Chronic Bronchitis, and Pneumonia.

Sleep Medicine

Specialized treatment for Sleep Apnea, Insomnia, Narcolepsy, and Restless Legs Syndrome to restore your rest.

Pulmonary Testing

Complete analysis in our laboratory, including Spirometry, lung volumes, and diffusion testing.

Pulmonary Function Laboratory

Dr. Kelly Wilton's office features a state-of-the-art on-site pulmonary physiology laboratory, allowing for quick and accurate diagnostics without the need to refer patients to external centers.

  • Simple and complex Spirometry.

  • Lung Volume Measurement (Plethysmography).

  • Diffusion Capacity Testing (DLCO).

  • Bronchial Provocation Testing (Methacholine Challenge).
